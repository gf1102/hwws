Our Story

Five women who worked together in a Harlem region of West 119th Street through West 124th Street, 5th Ave. – Frederick Douglas Blvd, beginning 1993 through 2004, met in April 2022 to fellowship with each other after 20 years. The purpose was simple: catch up with each others lives and see what we’d been doing over the years. We soon realized that there were so many collective memories, stories lessons, and authentic knowledge among the group: over 300 years. As we talked and enjoyed personal and collaborative stories, over a fabulous meal, we realized our collective body of knowledge should be shared with others. Essentially, our grassroots community organizing stories, lessons and knowledge should be shared with others in a way that support neighborhood change. Hence, we set our first meeting to develop a mission, purpose and plan for our work.
